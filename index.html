About

Dot Journal is a privacy-focused bullet journaling app that keeps your data on your device. Track tasks, events, habits, and moods with complete privacy control.

Features

  • Tasks, events, habits, and mood tracking
  • iOS home screen widgets
  • Apple Watch app with bidirectional sync
  • Optional iCloud sync (in your private iCloud)
  • Data export and backup
